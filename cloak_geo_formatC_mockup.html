Button colour — what the research says: there is no universal winner; the driver is contrast + context. Blue wins most general A/B tests (trust), but on this dark page it half-blends into the brand palette. Red/magenta drive urgency & action (T-Mobile, bouncy.ai); green reads “go / positive”. For a flirty OF funnel on a dark bg, a high-contrast magenta is the strongest single pick — with green the best thing to A/B against it. (Recommendation: ship magenta, test green next.)
